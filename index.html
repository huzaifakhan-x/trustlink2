About Trust Link & Co (Pvt) Ltd

In today's fast-paced property market, finding a trusted partner to guide you through investments, sales, and development projects is essential. That's where Trust Link & Co (Pvt) Ltd steps in — a name built on reliability, transparency, and customer-first service.

Based in Lahore, Pakistan, Trust Link & Co (Pvt) Ltd is a private limited company specializing in real estate solutions, project marketing, and property consultancy. With years of experience and a growing presence across Pakistan and the UAE, our mission is to make property investment secure and profitable for every client.

Our Services

We provide a complete range of real estate solutions tailored to our clients' needs, including:

  • Residential and Commercial Property Consultancy
  • Real Estate Marketing Campaigns for Housing Projects
  • Overseas Investment Opportunities
  • End-to-End Support, from Documentation to Handover

Why Choose Trust Link?

We don't just sell property — we build long-term relationships. Here's why clients choose us:

  • Experience: A proven track record of handling projects with professionalism.
  • Transparency: Clear communication, fair dealing, and legal compliance at every step.
  • Global Outlook: Serving clients in Pakistan and abroad, with dedicated lines for UAE-based investors.
  • Client-Centered: Personalized guidance designed to match your goals.
